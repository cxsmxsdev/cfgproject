Neptune

Some facts about Neptune

  • Neptune is the eigth planet from the Sun

  • It's more than 30 times as far from the Sun as Earth is

  • Neptune has 14 known moons

  • Neptune has six rings, but they're very hard to see

  • It is dark, cold and very windy

  • Its atmosphere is made of hydrogen, helium, and methane - the methane gives Neptune its blue color
