Mars

Some facts about Mars

  • Mars is the fourth planet from the Sun

  • It is half the size of Earth

  • It's red because of rusty iron in the ground

  • There are signs of ancient floods on Mars, but now water mostly exists in icy dirt and thin clouds

  • Similar to Earth, Mars has seasons, polar ice caps, volcanoes, canyons, and weather

  • It has a very thin atmosphere made of carbon dioxide, nitrogen, and argon
