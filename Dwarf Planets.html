Dwarf Planets

There are 5 dwarf planets in our solar system...
  • Pluto is a dwarf planet in the Kuiper Belt

  • Ceres is the largest object in the asteroid belt between Mars and Jupiter

  • Makemake is the second-brightest object in the Kuiper Belt as seen from Earth

  • Haumea is one of the fastest rotating large objects in our solar system

  • Eris is one of the largest known dwarf planets in our solar system
